About the Project

A simple way to understand and manage your money.

Expense Tracker is a web-based personal finance application designed to help users record income, track expenses, set monthly budgets, and analyse spending habits through clear visual reports.

The goal of this project is to make money management easier for everyday users by replacing manual tracking with a simple, secure, and user-friendly digital system.
